July 8, 2021

Moved the website (nh-voice.com).

June 18, 2021

The English title of the project was changed to "Graft the Voices".

May, 2021

題名を《声を接ぐ》に変更。ウェブサイト（grafthevoice.starfree.jp）を作成。

The title of the project was changed to "Graft the Voice on the Voices". The website(grafthevoice.starfree.jp) was built.

April 20, 2021

録音方法を変更。（録音時の日付、時間、場所を囁き、昨日録音した声の音高を模倣し、母音「あ」を発声する。）

The recording way has chaged. (After whispering the date, time, and place at the time of recording, imitate the pitch of the voice recorded yesterday and utter the vowel "A".)

February 20-23, 2021

《A》という題名で、録音した声を使用したインスタレーションを展示。於「IAMAS2021 情報科学芸術大学院大学 [IAMMAS] 第19期生終了研究発表会・プロジェクト研究発表会」

An installation titled "A" that uses voice recordings. @ "IAMAS2021 Graduation and Project Reseach Exhibition", Gifu, Japan

September 9, 2020

声の録音を始める。（録音時の日付、時間、場所を囁き、音叉の音を頼りにA（110ヘルツ）の音高で母音「あ」を発声する。）

Voice recording started. (After whispering the date, time, and place at the time of recording, the vowel "A" is uttered at the pitch of A (110 Hz), relying on the sound of the tuning fork.)

January 3, 1992

愛知県にて生まれる。

The artist was born in Aichi, Japan.
